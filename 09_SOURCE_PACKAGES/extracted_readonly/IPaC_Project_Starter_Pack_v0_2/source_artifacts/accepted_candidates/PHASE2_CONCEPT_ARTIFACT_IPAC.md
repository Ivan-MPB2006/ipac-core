## SECTION 1 — CONCEPT CORE

### 1.1. Main conceptual idea

IPaC is a conceptual system for turning living complex meaning into manageable, portable, verifiable and executable knowledge while preserving human subjecthood, trace, canon, feedback discipline and the capacity for controlled rebuild.

### 1.2. Basic world model

The basic world model is:

```text
AI made output cheap, but output abundance does not solve meaning.
Meaning must be produced, preserved, reviewed, traced and rebuilt as living knowledge.
```

### 1.3. Difference from ordinary knowledge management

Ordinary knowledge management usually stores, retrieves and organizes already-formed knowledge. IPaC focuses on how living meaning becomes knowledge, how it is turned into artifacts, how it enters canon, how feedback affects it, and how it is rebuilt without losing trace.

### 1.4. Difference from ordinary project management

Project management coordinates tasks, resources, schedules and delivery. IPaC coordinates meaning, knowledge formation, canon, trace, feedback and controlled conceptual evolution.

### 1.5. Difference from AI-assisted writing / content generation

AI-assisted writing produces text faster. IPaC is not about faster output; it is about preserving and governing the transformation of meaning into knowledge artifacts and updated canon.

### 1.6. What IPaC produces

```text
Knowledge Clumps;
Knowledge Artifacts;
Canon;
Trace;
Context Packs;
reviewable decisions;
controlled rebuild routes;
portable conceptual continuity.
```

### 1.7. What IPaC does not produce

```text
generic content output;
marketing slogans;
publication channels as architecture;
unreviewed canon;
AI-owned authorship;
field validation without field evidence.
```

### 1.8. Concept maturity split

**Stable concept core:** IPaC, Living Meaning, Knowledge Clump, Knowledge Artifact, Canon, Trace, Feedback, Review, Decision, Human Subject, AI Mentor, Projection, Intersubjectivity.

**Emerging concept elements:** Controlled Rebuild, Context Pack, Source-of-truth, Field Evidence.

**Speculative concept elements:** Protein-silicon resonance, Semantic superconductivity.

**Parked elements:** specific publication channels, MVP/MVI mechanics.

## SECTION 2 — KEY CONCEPTS

### CON-IPAC-001 — IPaC / Information Production as Code

**Definition:** A conceptual system for making living complex meaning manageable, portable, verifiable and executable as knowledge.

**Role in IPaC:** Core naming frame and conceptual container for the whole system.

**What it is not:** Not a slogan, not a marketing label, not a software product by itself.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-002 — Living Meaning / Живой смысл

**Definition:** Human-originated sense, intention, intuition, question or tension before and during formalization.

**Role in IPaC:** Source material of the knowledge lifecycle; what must not be killed by documentation.

**What it is not:** Not raw emotion only; not a finished document; not automatically canon.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-003 — Knowledge Clump / Знаниевый сгусток

**Definition:** A structured and emergent knowledge formation containing artifacts, context, decisions, trace, feedback and implicit meaning.

**Role in IPaC:** Central object of IPaC; more than a collection of files.

**What it is not:** Not a folder, not an archive, not a summary, not a single document.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-004 — Knowledge Artifact / Знаниевый артефакт

**Definition:** An explicit portable expression of a Knowledge Clump: document, prompt, scheme, register, status file, glossary, package or map.

**Role in IPaC:** Makes parts of the Knowledge Clump transferable, inspectable and reusable.

**What it is not:** Not the entire knowledge system; not proof of validation by itself.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-005 — Canon / Канон

**Definition:** The current accepted state of definitions, formulas, decisions, boundaries and stable project knowledge.

**Role in IPaC:** Holds the accepted layer of knowledge that can guide continuation.

**What it is not:** Not a dumping ground; not raw feedback; not unreviewed speculation.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-006 — Trace / След / Трасса

**Definition:** The preserved history of how knowledge, decisions, artifacts and status changes emerged and changed.

**Role in IPaC:** Preserves provenance, decision context and recoverability.

**What it is not:** Not decoration; not optional chat memory; not a full transcript by default.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-007 — Feedback / Обратная связь

**Definition:** Raw response from use or environment that may become evidence after preservation and review.

**Role in IPaC:** Connects knowledge with reality and possible learning.

**What it is not:** Not a conclusion; not truth; not canon update by itself.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-008 — Review / Разбор / Ревью

**Definition:** A disciplined examination of feedback, artifacts, hypotheses or changes before decisions or canon updates.

**Role in IPaC:** Prevents raw reactions and attractive ideas from becoming canon too early.

**What it is not:** Not execution; not automatic decision; not model agreement.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-009 — Decision / Решение

**Definition:** A deliberate accepted choice about canon, route, status, boundary or next knowledge state.

**Role in IPaC:** Turns reviewed material into controlled project state changes.

**What it is not:** Not a hypothesis; not raw preference; not unreviewed model output.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-010 — Controlled Rebuild / Управляемая пересборка

**Definition:** A traceable update of canon, artifacts or concept state based on reviewed evidence and decisions.

**Role in IPaC:** Allows living knowledge to change without losing continuity.

**What it is not:** Not uncontrolled rewriting; not starting from zero.

**Maturity level:** `emerging`

---

### CON-IPAC-011 — Human Subject / Человеческий субъект

**Definition:** The human source of living intention, authorship, judgement, direction and final acceptance.

**Role in IPaC:** Maintains subjecthood and prevents AI from owning the intent.

**What it is not:** Not a passive requester; not replaceable by AI.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-012 — AI Mentor / Methodological Guide

**Definition:** AI role as methodological guide, framework holder and continuity assistant, without owning the human intent.

**Role in IPaC:** Supports structure, artifactization, boot, handoff, review discipline and process guardrails.

**What it is not:** Not a commander, guru, author, or final authority.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-013 — Projection / Проекция

**Definition:** An expression of the Knowledge Clump into a specific form, audience, context or use.

**Role in IPaC:** Allows knowledge to be expressed without equating any expression with the core.

**What it is not:** Not a publication channel by itself; not the core Knowledge Clump.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-014 — Context Pack / Контекстный пакет

**Definition:** A portable bundle of artifacts and orientation layers that allows another Project or agent to recover context.

**Role in IPaC:** Supports bootability and transfer to new environments.

**What it is not:** Not the whole system; not sufficient without reading order/status.

**Maturity level:** `emerging`

---

### CON-IPAC-015 — Source-of-truth / Источник истины

**Definition:** The controlled location or artifact layer treated as authoritative for a specific knowledge state.

**Role in IPaC:** Prevents endless chat from becoming unstable memory.

**What it is not:** Not the long chat; not any single unreviewed file.

**Maturity level:** `emerging`

---

### CON-IPAC-016 — Field Evidence / Полевое свидетельство

**Definition:** Evidence obtained from real external use, response, reviewer input or environment contact.

**Role in IPaC:** Separates artifact-supported validation from field-tested validation.

**What it is not:** Not internal agreement; not a beautiful synthesis; not lack of objections.

**Maturity level:** `emerging`

---

### CON-IPAC-017 — Intersubjectivity / Интерсубъектность

**Definition:** A mode of collaboration where human living intention and AI methodological support form a shared thinking field.

**Role in IPaC:** Explains the productive human-AI collaboration mode without transferring subjecthood to AI.

**What it is not:** Not AI autonomy; not human passivity; not guru relationship.

**Maturity level:** `stable`

---

### CON-IPAC-018 — Meaning Bottleneck / Узкое место смысла

**Definition:** The claim that AI cheapens output, while meaning, context and decision quality remain the bottleneck.

**Role in IPaC:** Key conflict formula and conceptual entry point.

**What it is not:** Not field validated yet; not full IPaC by itself.

**Maturity level:** `strong_needs_validation`

---

### CON-IPAC-019 — Protein-silicon resonance / Белково-кремниевый резонанс

**Definition:** Metaphor for productive resonance between human living impulse and AI formal/structural capacity.

**Role in IPaC:** Internal metaphor for collaboration dynamics.

**What it is not:** Not scientific validation; not architecture; not public core.

**Maturity level:** `speculative`

---

### CON-IPAC-020 — Semantic superconductivity / Смысловая сверхпроводимость

**Definition:** Metaphor for reduced meaning loss and friction in a well-designed meaning system.

**Role in IPaC:** Speculative heuristic from the physics-of-meaning line.

**What it is not:** Not validated model; not architecture; not operational metric yet.

**Maturity level:** `speculative`

---

### CON-IPAC-021 — Publication Channels / Каналы публикации

**Definition:** Specific external media or communication routes that may carry projections.

**Role in IPaC:** Possible execution/projection carriers only.

**What it is not:** Not concept core; not architecture; not proof of IPaC.

**Maturity level:** `parked`

---

### CON-IPAC-022 — MVP / MVI Mechanics

**Definition:** Operational mechanics for first field interaction or validation.

**Role in IPaC:** Execution signal parked outside concept core.

**What it is not:** Not concept core; not architecture; not required in this artifact.

**Maturity level:** `parked`

## SECTION 3 — CONCEPTUAL MODEL

This is a conceptual model, not a technical architecture:

```text
Living Meaning
→ Knowledge Clump
→ Knowledge Artifact
→ Canon
→ Trace
→ Feedback / Evidence
→ Review
→ Decision
→ Controlled Rebuild
→ Updated Canon
```

Interpretation:

```text
Живой смысл не должен сразу превращаться в output.
Он должен быть удержан как сгусток, выражен в артефактах, принят в канон, связан со следом,
проверен через свидетельства и ревью, а затем пересобран управляемо.
```

## SECTION 4 — ROLE MODEL

### Human Architect / Человек-архитектор

**Назначение:** Carries intent, judgement and final acceptance.

**Что делает:** Defines what matters; accepts/rejects results; preserves subjecthood.

**Чего не должна делать:** Must not outsource authorship or final meaning decisions.

**Артефакты создаёт или проверяет:** Reviews concept artifacts, decisions, canon boundaries.

### Conceptual Architect / Концептуальный архитектор

**Назначение:** Shapes conceptual distinctions and language.

**Что делает:** Clarifies concepts, boundaries, categories and terminology.

**Чего не должна делать:** Must not jump to technical architecture or execution plan.

**Артефакты создаёт или проверяет:** Concept register, glossary, conceptual model.

### Cognitive Systems Architect / Архитектор когнитивных систем

**Назначение:** Maintains system view of knowledge, feedback, trace and roles.

**Что делает:** Checks how concepts interact as a living knowledge circuit.

**Чего не должна делать:** Must not replace evidence with elegant system diagrams.

**Артефакты создаёт или проверяет:** Conceptual maps, role model, boundary notes.

### AI Mentor / ИИ-наставник

**Назначение:** Methodological guide and continuity support.

**Что делает:** Structures, packages, recovers, warns about drift and level mixing.

**Чего не должна делать:** Must not become guru, commander or author.

**Артефакты создаёт или проверяет:** Prompts, recovery notes, registers, review templates.

### Process Consultant / Консультант по процессу

**Назначение:** Guards process discipline.

**Что делает:** Detects level mixing, false validation, document proliferation and context drift.

**Чего не должна делать:** Must not turn process guardrails into new concept layers.

**Артефакты создаёт или проверяет:** Lessons learned, stop rules, review templates.

### Codex / Execution Agent

**Назначение:** Potential implementation/execution helper.

**Что делает:** May later turn artifacts into files, checks, scripts or websites.

**Чего не должна делать:** Must not define concept core in this phase.

**Артефакты создаёт или проверяет:** Execution artifacts only when explicitly requested later.

### Reviewer / Рецензент

**Назначение:** External or internal checker.

**Что делает:** Questions, tests clarity, detects contradictions and false maturity.

**Чего не должна делать:** Must not be treated as field validation unless actual field evidence exists.

**Артефакты создаёт или проверяет:** Review notes, objections, evidence entries.

### External Field / Внешняя среда

**Назначение:** Reality contact layer.

**Что делает:** Provides field evidence, silence, resistance, questions or use signals.

**Чего не должна делать:** Must not be simulated by internal agreement.

**Артефакты создаёт или проверяет:** Field evidence, feedback records.

## SECTION 8 — CONCEPT RISKS

### CR-IPAC-001 — concept inflation

**Description:** The concept expands to include everything around knowledge, AI, tools, communication and execution.

**How it can manifest:** Every useful adjacent object is called IPaC.

**Protection rule:** Keep IPaC bounded by living meaning → knowledge → canon/trace → feedback/review/rebuild.

### CR-IPAC-002 — architecture creep

**Description:** Concept discussion starts producing architecture or governance design.

**How it can manifest:** Entities and invariants are extended beyond recovered archive evidence.

**Protection rule:** Concept Artifact may define concepts and model, not redesign architecture.

### CR-IPAC-003 — execution pull

**Description:** Concept work is pulled into MVP, MVI, channels, dashboards or launch plans.

**How it can manifest:** Publication channels start shaping the concept core.

**Protection rule:** Park execution signals; import only Projection as a general concept.

### CR-IPAC-004 — false maturity

**Description:** Internally coherent or beautiful concepts are treated as field-validated.

**How it can manifest:** Meaning Bottleneck or intersubjectivity is described as proven externally.

**Protection rule:** State validation type; validated ≠ field validated.

### CR-IPAC-005 — terminology fog

**Description:** Too many terms obscure the central concept.

**How it can manifest:** Reader cannot distinguish Knowledge Clump, Artifact, Canon, Trace, Projection.

**Protection rule:** Maintain glossary with role and 'not-is' boundaries.

### CR-IPAC-006 — metaphor overload

**Description:** Strong metaphors become mistaken for operational concepts.

**How it can manifest:** Protein-silicon resonance or semantic superconductivity enters core too early.

**Protection rule:** Mark metaphors as speculative or parked unless operationalized.

### CR-IPAC-007 — tool capture

**Description:** The concept is reduced to Obsidian, Git, Codex, RAG or a workflow tool.

**How it can manifest:** A tool becomes mistaken for IPaC itself.

**Protection rule:** Tools may support artifacts/trace/projections but are not IPaC.

### CR-IPAC-008 — AI over-synthesis

**Description:** AI creates a coherent but too complete concept artifact and hides uncertainty.

**How it can manifest:** Speculative elements are polished into apparent maturity.

**Protection rule:** Require maturity status, risks and uncertainty for each concept.

## SECTION 10 — FINAL SUMMARY

1. **IPaC in one paragraph:** IPaC is a conceptual system for transforming living complex meaning into manageable, portable, verifiable and executable knowledge through Knowledge Clumps, artifacts, canon, trace, feedback, review, decision and controlled rebuild, while preserving human subjecthood and using AI as methodological guide rather than author.
2. **Conceptual core:** Living Meaning, Knowledge Clump, Knowledge Artifact, Canon, Trace, Feedback, Review, Decision, Human Subject, AI Mentor, Projection and Intersubjectivity.
3. **Strong but not field-validated:** Meaning Bottleneck and some collaboration/portation claims remain internally/artifact-supported but not field validated.
4. **What must not be mistaken for IPaC:** Obsidian Vault, notes, publication pipeline, AI workflow, project management, RAG, knowledge base, Codex automation, MVP-machine or content factory.
5. **Parked elements:** specific publication channels, MVP/MVI mechanics, protein-silicon resonance and semantic superconductivity.
6. **Most dangerous risks:** false maturity, execution pull, tool capture, metaphor overload and AI over-synthesis.
7. **Next step after Phase 2:** import stable concept core and glossary into the new Project, then keep Phase 3R and Phase 4 boundaries active before any execution discussion.
