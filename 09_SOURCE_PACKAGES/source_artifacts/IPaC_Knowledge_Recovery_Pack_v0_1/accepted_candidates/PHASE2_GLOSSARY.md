# IPaC Concept Glossary

## IPaC / Information Production as Code

A conceptual system for making living complex meaning manageable, portable, verifiable and executable as knowledge.

**Role:** Core naming frame and conceptual container for the whole system.

**Not:** Not a slogan, not a marketing label, not a software product by itself.

## Living Meaning / Живой смысл

Human-originated sense, intention, intuition, question or tension before and during formalization.

**Role:** Source material of the knowledge lifecycle; what must not be killed by documentation.

**Not:** Not raw emotion only; not a finished document; not automatically canon.

## Knowledge Clump / Знаниевый сгусток

A structured and emergent knowledge formation containing artifacts, context, decisions, trace, feedback and implicit meaning.

**Role:** Central object of IPaC; more than a collection of files.

**Not:** Not a folder, not an archive, not a summary, not a single document.

## Knowledge Artifact / Знаниевый артефакт

An explicit portable expression of a Knowledge Clump: document, prompt, scheme, register, status file, glossary, package or map.

**Role:** Makes parts of the Knowledge Clump transferable, inspectable and reusable.

**Not:** Not the entire knowledge system; not proof of validation by itself.

## Canon / Канон

The current accepted state of definitions, formulas, decisions, boundaries and stable project knowledge.

**Role:** Holds the accepted layer of knowledge that can guide continuation.

**Not:** Not a dumping ground; not raw feedback; not unreviewed speculation.

## Trace / След / Трасса

The preserved history of how knowledge, decisions, artifacts and status changes emerged and changed.

**Role:** Preserves provenance, decision context and recoverability.

**Not:** Not decoration; not optional chat memory; not a full transcript by default.

## Feedback / Обратная связь

Raw response from use or environment that may become evidence after preservation and review.

**Role:** Connects knowledge with reality and possible learning.

**Not:** Not a conclusion; not truth; not canon update by itself.

## Review / Разбор / Ревью

A disciplined examination of feedback, artifacts, hypotheses or changes before decisions or canon updates.

**Role:** Prevents raw reactions and attractive ideas from becoming canon too early.

**Not:** Not execution; not automatic decision; not model agreement.

## Decision / Решение

A deliberate accepted choice about canon, route, status, boundary or next knowledge state.

**Role:** Turns reviewed material into controlled project state changes.

**Not:** Not a hypothesis; not raw preference; not unreviewed model output.

## Controlled Rebuild / Управляемая пересборка

A traceable update of canon, artifacts or concept state based on reviewed evidence and decisions.

**Role:** Allows living knowledge to change without losing continuity.

**Not:** Not uncontrolled rewriting; not starting from zero.

## Human Subject / Человеческий субъект

The human source of living intention, authorship, judgement, direction and final acceptance.

**Role:** Maintains subjecthood and prevents AI from owning the intent.

**Not:** Not a passive requester; not replaceable by AI.

## AI Mentor / Methodological Guide

AI role as methodological guide, framework holder and continuity assistant, without owning the human intent.

**Role:** Supports structure, artifactization, boot, handoff, review discipline and process guardrails.

**Not:** Not a commander, guru, author, or final authority.

## Projection / Проекция

An expression of the Knowledge Clump into a specific form, audience, context or use.

**Role:** Allows knowledge to be expressed without equating any expression with the core.

**Not:** Not a publication channel by itself; not the core Knowledge Clump.

## Context Pack / Контекстный пакет

A portable bundle of artifacts and orientation layers that allows another Project or agent to recover context.

**Role:** Supports bootability and transfer to new environments.

**Not:** Not the whole system; not sufficient without reading order/status.

## Source-of-truth / Источник истины

The controlled location or artifact layer treated as authoritative for a specific knowledge state.

**Role:** Prevents endless chat from becoming unstable memory.

**Not:** Not the long chat; not any single unreviewed file.

## Field Evidence / Полевое свидетельство

Evidence obtained from real external use, response, reviewer input or environment contact.

**Role:** Separates artifact-supported validation from field-tested validation.

**Not:** Not internal agreement; not a beautiful synthesis; not lack of objections.

## Intersubjectivity / Интерсубъектность

A mode of collaboration where human living intention and AI methodological support form a shared thinking field.

**Role:** Explains the productive human-AI collaboration mode without transferring subjecthood to AI.

**Not:** Not AI autonomy; not human passivity; not guru relationship.

## Meaning Bottleneck / Узкое место смысла

The claim that AI cheapens output, while meaning, context and decision quality remain the bottleneck.

**Role:** Key conflict formula and conceptual entry point.

**Not:** Not field validated yet; not full IPaC by itself.
