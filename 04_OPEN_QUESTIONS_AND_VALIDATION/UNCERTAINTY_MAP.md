# 03 — Open Questions and Uncertainty Map

## Purpose

Phase 6 protects IPaC from false maturity.

Open questions are not weakness. They are the basis for disciplined validation.

## Recovered uncertainty layer

- Open questions: 22
- Contradictions: 10
- Assumptions: 9
- Validation gaps: 6
- Terminology risks: 10
- Parked questions: 10

## Top unresolved questions

1. Does IPaC answer a real external need, or mainly an internal intellectual/systemic pain?
2. Can living meaning be translated into manageable artifacts without killing its living core?
3. Is the Canon / Trace / Review / Decision / Controlled Rebuild circuit workable in practice?
4. What is the boundary between IPaC and Knowledge Engineering?
5. Can Obsidian / Git / Project serve as stable memory without tool capture?

## Active vs parked

Active open questions influence the next validation cycle.

Parked questions should not pull the Project into premature architecture or execution.
